Click the record button to record your occupation. To submit your recording to Sitecore, enter your email address and click the "Submit to Sitecore" button.

If you submit your recording to Sitecore and your recording mentioned one of the recognized occupations, then if you browse to Sitecore and supply your email to the form on the homepage then you should see personalized content for your occupation.
